Get to Know Me Better

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics Education from Sriwijaya University, Indonesia. I'm now furthering my studies with a Master's degree in Mathematics Education from Terbuka University in the same country.

I am committed to fostering a positive and inclusive learning environment where every student feels valued and empowered to succeed. I believe in the power of hands-on, inquiry-based learning to spark curiosity and deepen understanding.

My passion for mathematics education extends beyond the classroom. I am particularly interested in exploring the role of technology in mathematics instruction, utilizing innovative tools to enhance student learning experiences while ensuring accessibility and inclusivity. Additionally, I am dedicated to designing culturally relevant curriculum materials that resonate with diverse student backgrounds, fostering an inclusive learning environment where all students feel represented and valued. Furthermore, I am enthusiastic about promoting interdisciplinary connections in the teaching of mathematics, recognizing the interconnectedness of mathematics with other fields and empowering students to make meaningful connections between mathematical concepts and real-world applications.
